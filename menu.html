Welcome to Baseball For Bucks, a variation of Fantasy Baseball, where you take a trip down memory lane, and manage your own historical MLB team.

So, how does it work?

Choose a team and year from the Step 1 pane. Their general lineup will appear in the Step 2 pane. You can drag each batter to a different position in the lineup, if you think the team will do better that way.
Once you have chosen your winning lineup, click "Play Ball" to simulate the actual season for that team, but with your lineup.
Yes, the computer will simulate their entire schedule of that year, against their real opponents, just with your lineup.
In a few seconds, you will get a message of your record.

Currently, this site is in BETA. But once we fix all the bugs, we will move to the make-money version, where you can make lots of money with the right lineup.
It'll cost just $1 to play a teams, and whoever has the highest winning percentage at the end of each calendar week, will win 50% of the pot.

This is NOT illegal gambling. The only thing that's illegal about gambling is if you can't control the outcome at all, such as a slot machine.
But here, you have to know the game of baseball, study the team and players, and make a decision for a winning lineup.
