Welcome to Baseball For Bucks, a variation of Fantasy Baseball, where you take a trip down memory lane, and manage your own historical MLB team.

So, how does it work?

Choose a team and year from the Step 1 pane. Their general lineup will appear in the Step 2 pane. You can drag each batter to a different position in the lineup, if you think the team will do better that way.
When you are finished, click the blue right arrow to add this team to your lineups in the Step 3 pane.
Repeat this step for four more teams. (They may be the same team if you wish.)

Once you have chosen your five teams, click "Play Ball" to simulate the actual seasons for those teams, but with your lineup.
Yes, they will play their actual schedule of that year, against their real opponents, just with your lineup.
In a few seconds, you will get a message of your average win/loss record.

Currently, this site is in BETA. But once we find and fix all the bugs, we will move to the make-money version, where you can make lots of money with the right lineups.
It's cost $5 to play all five teams, and whoever has the highest winning percentage at the end of each calendar week, will win 50% of the pot.
So, if 500 people play this game in a week, the pool will be $2500, and if you win, you'll earn $1250.
This is NOT illegal gambling. The only thing that's illegal about gambling is if you can't control the outcome at all, such as a slot machine.
But here, you have to know the game of baseball, study the team and players, and make a decision for a winning lineup.
